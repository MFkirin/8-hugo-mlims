Solo

Solo is a beatbox battle category involving an individual beatboxer performing a routine. It is the most common category found in events.

Format

In the first round, each participant makes a 4-minutes presentation (Showcase), where the judges evaluated whether he is fit for the second round. The 16 best beatboxers move on to the next stage.

In the second phase, the 16 beatboxers face off in a knockout stage. In each battle, the beatboxers have two rounds of 90 seconds each.
